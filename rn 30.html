Hyundai RN30

The Hyundai RN30 is a high-performance concept car born from the brand’s motorsport heritage. With aggressive aerodynamics, wide stance, and powerful turbocharged engine, it's built for the track yet hints at future N models.

  • 🏁 2.0L turbocharged engine with 380 horsepower
  • 🧪 Lightweight materials and racing chassis
  • 🎯 Advanced aerodynamics and downforce design
  • 🚀 Concept inspiration for Hyundai N performance line
