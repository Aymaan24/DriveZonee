Ferrari 812 Competizione

Car Info :

The Ferrari 812 Competizione is a Ferrari masterpiece, designed to be the most extreme and advanced version of the 812 Superfast series. Launched in 2021 as a limited production car (only 999 units for the hardtop version and 599 units for the open-topped "Aperta"), it embodies the pinnacle of V12 engine technology in the world of sports cars.

  • Engine Type: 6.5-liter V12 (the most powerful V12 engine in Ferrari history to date).
  • Power: 830 hp at 9,250 rpm , Torque: 692 Nm at 7,000 rpm.
  • Performance: Acceleration (0-100 km/h) in 2.85 seconds , Top Speed: +340 km/h , Vehicle Weight: 1,487 kg (lighter than the 812 Superfast).
  • Transmission : 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT) with rear-wheel drive.
  • Base Price (Coupe):$550,000 - $600,000 USD (before options & taxes)
