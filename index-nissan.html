About

Nissan Motors has served as a pioneer in the automobile industry in Japan since its inception, achieving this level of development through its exclusive technologies. Nissan Motors then gained fame as a global leader in the automotive industry, exporting cars bearing its brand to more than 190 countries. It also has production bases all over the world. The company is working to expand the automotive market through the large-scale production of electric-powered cars such as the Nissan LEAF, and the launch of INFINITI, a luxury brand, in addition to working to advance the future of the mobility industry towards a better future based on its leadership in innovation in connectivity and autonomous driving technologies. It also seeks to find better solutions to serve humanity by providing technical innovations aimed at “enriching people’s lives.”
