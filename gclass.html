Mercedes G CLASS

-Engine?

G 500 / G 550 (varies by region): Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo Power: Approx. 422 horsepower Torque: 610 Nm AMG G63: Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo (AMG-tuned) Power: 577 horsepower (430 kW) Torque: 850 Nm Cleck here

-Performance & Speed:

G 500 / G 550: 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): Approx. 5.9 seconds Top speed: 210 km/h (electronically limited) AMG G63: 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): Approx. 4.5 seconds Top speed: 220 km/h standard Up to 240 km/h with the AMG Driver’s Package Despite its large size and weight (over 2.5 tons), the G-Class delivers strong acceleration thanks to its powerful engine and 9-speed automatic transmission.

- Safety?

The Mercedes G-Class is equipped with advanced safety and driver-assistance systems: Driver Assistance Features: Blind Spot Assist Lane Keeping Assist Active Brake Assist Emergency braking system 360° Surround View Cameras Front and Rear Parking Sensors Adaptive Cruise Control Structural & Passive Safety: Extremely rigid ladder-frame chassis Multiple airbags (front, side, curtain) Electronic Stability Control (ESP) Anti-Rollover System Strong off-road protection with 3 locking differentials.
