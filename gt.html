-Engine?

AMG GT Coupe / Roadster (Base Models): Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo Power: 469–523 horsepower (depending on model) Torque: 630–670 Nm AMG GT R / GT R Pro: Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo Power: 577 horsepower Torque: 700 Nm AMG GT Black Series (Track-focused version): Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo (Flat-plane crank) Power: 720 horsepower Torque: 800 Nm. -Performance & Speed:

AMG GT Coupe (Base): 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): 3.9 seconds Top speed: 304 km/h (189 mph) AMG GT R: 0–100 km/h: 3.6 seconds Top speed: 318 km/h (198 mph) AMG GT Black Series: 0–100 km/h: 3.2 seconds Top speed: 325 km/h (202 mph) All models feature rear-wheel drive, a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, and AMG Ride Control suspension

- Safety Features?

Despite being a performance car, the AMG GT comes with several safety systems: Active Safety: Active Brake Assist Blind Spot Monitoring Lane Keeping Assist Adaptive Cruise Control Collision Prevention Assist Attention Assist (Driver fatigue monitoring) Rearview Camera (with optional 360° view in some models) Passive Safety: Multiple airbags (front, side, knee) High-strength safety cell Stability and Traction Control Performance braking system with optional carbon-ceramic brakes
