NISSAN GTR 2025

About Car:

The 2025 Nissan GT-R is one of the most iconic sports cars offered by Nissan, and belongs to the high-performance coupe category. Here are the latest specifications and highlights for the 2025 model. The 2025 Nissan GT-R is available in multiple trims including Premium, T-Spec, and NISMO, each offering unique features to suit different driving preferences and performance need

  • The car is equipped with a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that delivers up to 600 horsepower.
  • It has a torque of 156 Newton metres, and is linked to a 4-speed automatic transmission.
  • Operates with an all-wheel-drive (AWD) system for maximum grip and stability
  • The GT-R reaches a top speed of around 315 km/h
  • The car accelerates from zero to 100 km/h in approximately 2.9 seconds
  • Fuel consumption is estimated at around 11.8 liters per 100 km in combined driving conditions.
